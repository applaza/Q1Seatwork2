Characters Of Noli Me Tangere

Image Name Description
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra, San Diego's richest man. He spent seven years studying in Europe and is Maria Clara's lover.
Maria Clara The most beautiful lady in San Diego. Kapitan Tiago's only child/daughter. Was raised by her father and aunt, Tiya Isabel.
Kapitan Tiago His full name is Don Santiago de los Santos. Maria Clara's so-called father. He's one of San Diego's wealthiest people and the host of the dinner in Chapter 3.
Tiya Isabel Is Kapitan Tiago's sister and Maria Clara's aunt. Despite the fact that she is Maria Clara's aunt, Maria Clara sees her as if she is her mother for Tiya Isabel reared her.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is a priest of the 'franciscano' order. He is Maria Clara's true father, as he raped her mother.He is also opposed to his real daughter's connection with Ibarra.
